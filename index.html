building=yes is a searchable and linkable index of every single "way" tagged building=yes in OpenStreetMap (OSM).

Currently there are just over 26 million buildings in the database. (If you don't limit the search to just buildings tagged yes there are actually 49 million buildings in OSM as of early 2012!)

This is the code that runs the site.

This project is maintained by straup
